# Interview Answer — Explain the GDPR Compliance Lifecycle

The GDPR compliance lifecycle is a continuous set of structured steps organizations follow to ensure personal data is processed, stored, protected, and maintained properly.

The lifecycle consists of:
1. **Data Mapping & Discovery**  
2. **Gap Assessment**  
3. **Remediation & Controls**  
4. **Monitoring & Evidence**  
5. **Incident Response & Breach Management**  
6. **Continuous Improvement**

This process ensures compliance is maintained year after year, adapts to new risks, and keeps data protection effective and transparent.
