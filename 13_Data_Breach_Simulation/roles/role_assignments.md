# 👥 Incident Response Role Assignments

## Core Roles & Responsibilities

### 🧭 Incident Commander (IC)
- Coordinates entire response
- Decision authority
- Approves communication & containment

### 🛡️ Technical Lead (Forensics / IR)
- Investigates logs, endpoints, network data
- Identifies root cause
- Builds timeline of events

### 🗂️ Legal & Compliance Officer
- Assesses regulatory obligations (GDPR, NIS2)
- Approves notifications
- Ensures documentation is compliant

### 📞 Communications Lead (Internal & External)
- Prepares internal updates
- Handles press, customers, authorities
- Ensures message consistency

### 🙋 HR Lead
- Handles employee-related issues
- Trains & guides affected personnel

### 🤝 Vendor Manager
- Contacts external providers
- Coordinates breach response with third parties
