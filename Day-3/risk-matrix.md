# Risk Matrix (Likelihood × Impact)

This document displays the Risk Matrix used to calculate ratings in the Risk Register.

---

## 📊 Visual Risk Matrix

![Risk Matrix](./assets/risk-matrix.png)

---

## 📘 How to Use This Matrix

- **Step 1:** Assign *Likelihood* (Low, Medium, High)  
- **Step 2:** Assign *Impact* (Low, Medium, High)  
- **Step 3:** Cross-reference both values  
- **Step 4:** Select the resulting *Risk Rating*  

This matrix ensures consistency across all risk evaluations.

---

## ✔ Example Interpretation
- **High Likelihood × High Impact** → *Critical Risk*  
- **Medium Likelihood × Low Impact** → *Medium Risk*  
- **Low Likelihood × High Impact** → *Medium or High Risk (context-dependent)*
