# Mapping Process Steps

This mapping follows the standard industry methodology:

## Step 1 — Identify Target Frameworks
ISO 27001, SOC 2, PCI-DSS, NIST CSF, GDPR.

## Step 2 — Extract Controls
Controls were extracted from each framework’s primary source.

## Step 3 — Align Domains
Mapped high-level domains (Access Control, IR, Risk, etc.) between frameworks.

## Step 4 — Map Individual Controls
For each ISO control, equivalent NIST/PCI/GDPR/SOC 2 requirements were aligned.

## Step 5 — Assign Mapping Strength
- Exact Match  
- Partial Overlap  
- No Equivalent  

## Step 6 — Link Evidence
Create a unified evidence table showing documents/screenshots that satisfy multiple controls.

## Step 7 — Review and Validate
Final check for coverage consistency.
