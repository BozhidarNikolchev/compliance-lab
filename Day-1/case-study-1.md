# Case Study #1 — Customer Due Diligence (CDD)

## 1. Identity Verification
- ID verification result: Valid Slovak ID, not expired, and no signs of manipulation.
- Selfie match: Face clearly matches the ID photo.
- Proof of address: Swedish rental contract; name and address consistent with the customer profile.
- Concerns: None observed.

## 2. Sanctions / PEP / Adverse Media
- Sanctions result: No sanctions found.
- PEP status: Customer is not politically exposed.
- Media results: No adverse media or criminal activity reported.
- Notes: No inconsistencies identified.

## 3. Risk Assessment
- Risk level (Low/Medium/High): Low to Medium.
- Justification: Customer operates a normal‑risk EU e‑commerce business. All documents are valid, stated activity is consistent, and no high‑risk jurisdictions or negative indicators are present.

## 4. Final Decision
- Decision: Approve.
- Rationale: All KYC documentation is valid, no sanctions or PEP hits, the customer’s expected activity matches their occupation, and the risk level meets onboarding requirements.

## 5. Monitoring Notes
- Monitor for any sudden spikes in turnover or transfers to non‑EU high‑risk jurisdictions.
