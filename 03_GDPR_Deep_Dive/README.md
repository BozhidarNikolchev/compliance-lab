# Compliance Risk Management

This repository contains portfolio materials for Day 3 of the Compliance Analyst Program.  
It includes a fully developed Compliance Risk Register, a Risk Matrix visualization, and supporting documentation.

---

## 📌 Contents

### 1. **Risk Register**
A structured table containing:
- Identified compliance risks  
- Likelihood and impact scoring  
- Calculated rating  
- Existing and recommended controls  
- Control ownership and review dates  

Located in:  
`/risk-register.md`

---

### 2. **Risk Matrix (Likelihood × Impact)**
Visual diagram used to calculate risk ratings consistently.

Located in:  
`/risk-matrix.md`  
Image file in `/assets/risk-matrix.png`

### 3. **Supporting Files**
- MIT License  
- Documentation assets

## 🎯 Purpose of This Repository

This repo demonstrates:
- Understanding of regulatory and cybersecurity risks  
- Ability to perform structured risk assessments  
- Knowledge of mitigating controls  
- Familiarity with governance, risk & compliance (GRC) documentation  
- Ability to produce professional reporting artifacts  

All documents are designed for **interview portfolios, junior GRC roles, compliance analyst positions, and cybersecurity governance pathways**.
