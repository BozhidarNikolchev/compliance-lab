# Internal Audit Plan

## Scope
Access control, logging, asset management, risk management, incident response, vendor management.

## Objectives
- Validate control design & effectiveness  
- Confirm compliance with policies & standards  
- Identify gaps in governance & operations  

## Audit Criteria
- Organization policies  
- ISO 27001 / SOC 2 / NIST CSF  
- Legal requirements (GDPR)  

## Team Roles
- Lead Auditor  
- Technical Auditor  
- Observer / Scribe
