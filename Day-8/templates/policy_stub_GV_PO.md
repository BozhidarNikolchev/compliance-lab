# Policy Stub – GV.PO (Cyber Risk Management Policy)

**Purpose**: Establish principles, roles, and requirements for managing cyber risk.
**Scope**: Applies to all systems, data, and suppliers in scope.
**Roles**: Board, CISO, Service Owners, Third-Party Management.
**Requirements**:
- Policies are reviewed **annually** or upon material changes.
- **Risk appetite** is approved by the board.
- **SCRM**: Contract clauses, due diligence, and continuous monitoring.
**Compliance**: Violations are escalated via the incident process.
