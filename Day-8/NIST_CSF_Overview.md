
# NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) – Overview

## Purpose
A concise, professional overview of NIST CSF 2.0.

## Core Functions
- **Govern (GV)** – Governance, policy, oversight.
- **Identify (ID)** – Assets, risks, context.
- **Protect (PR)** – Safeguards, access control, training, data protection.
- **Detect (DE)** – Monitoring and anomaly detection.
- **Respond (RS)** – Incident handling, mitigation.
- **Recover (RC)** – Continuity & restoration.

## Profiles
Used to compare *Current* vs *Target* cybersecurity posture.

## Tiers
Four maturity levels representing cybersecurity governance.
