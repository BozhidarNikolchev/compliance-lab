Author: Bozhidar Nikolchev

## 🎯 Purpose  
Understand internal & external audit processes, evidence requirements, how controls are validated, and how audit findings are managed.

---

# ✅ 1. What Is an Audit?

## Internal Audit  
- Conducted by internal audit/compliance  
- Focus on policies, processes, controls, governance  
- Helps detect issues before external audit

## External Audit  
- Performed by certification bodies (ISO, SOC 2)  
- Requires strong evidence  
- Leads to certifications or compliance reports

---

# ✅ 2. Audit Types
### ✅ Document Audit  
Review of policies, procedures, control descriptions.

### ✅ Implementation Audit  
Verification of real configurations, logs, access, workflows.

### ✅ Surveillance Audit  
Annual check to maintain certification.

---

# ✅ 3. What Auditors Look For (Evidence)
- Screenshots  
- Log exports  
- Access control lists  
- Policies & procedures  
- Training records  
- Incident tickets  
- Vendor documentation  

---

# ✅ 4. Audit Workflow
1. **Define scope**  
2. **Collect documentation**  
3. **Evidence request list**  
4. **Interviews with owners**  
5. **Control testing**  
6. **Findings issued**  
7. **Corrective Action Plan (CAPA)**  

---

# ✅ 5. Internal Audit Checklist
- [ ] Security Policy  
- [ ] Access Control Policy  
- [ ] IR Plan  
- [ ] Logs retained properly  
- [ ] Training programs  
- [ ] Vendor risk reviewed  
- [ ] Backups + DR tested
