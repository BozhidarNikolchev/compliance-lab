
# Day 15 — Vendor Risk Management (VRM)

This folder contains all deliverables created for **Vendor Risk Management** of Compliance & Security Governance Program.

## 📌 Overview
Day 15 focuses on **Vendor Risk Management (VRM)** — a critical component of GRC, Compliance, and Privacy programs. The goal is to evaluate third‑party suppliers, assess their security and privacy posture, identify risks, and document the results.

This folder includes:
- ✅ A complete **Vendor Inventory**
- ✅ A **Vendor Security & Privacy Questionnaire Template** (ISO 27001 + GDPR aligned)
- ✅ A real **Vendor Risk Assessment** for HubSpot
- ✅ A **Risk Scoring Summary** used during the evaluation

## 📁 Files Included
### **1. Vendor_Inventory.md**
A structured list of vendors, classification by criticality, type of data processed, and operational notes.

### **2. Vendor_Questionnaire_Template.md**
A professional due‑diligence questionnaire used for assessing suppliers' security, privacy, and compliance practices.

### **3. Vendor_Risk_Assessment_HubSpot.md**
A full real‑world assessment of HubSpot based on:
- Certifications (SOC2, SOC3)
- GDPR compliance & DPA
- Security controls
- Infrastructure & data centers
- Incident response maturity
- 2026 security advisory

### **4. Risk_Scoring_Summary.md**
Explains the vendor scoring model and includes the HubSpot score calculation.
