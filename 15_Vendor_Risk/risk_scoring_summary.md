
# Risk Scoring Summary (Vendor Risk Management)

## Scoring Model
- Confidentiality Impact (C)
- Integrity Impact (I)
- Availability Impact (A)
- Compliance Risk (CR)

Scores:
- High = 3
- Medium = 2
- Low = 1

Final Score Formula: C + I + A + CR

Risk Levels:
- 4–6 = Low Risk
- 7–9 = Medium Risk
- 10–12 = High Risk

## HubSpot Example
- C = 2
- I = 1
- A = 1
- CR = 1

**Total Score = 5 → Low Risk**
